InSync Power

Creating Inverters Ready for Renewables

One of the biggest difficulties in the global effort to make energy production cleaner is the integration of direct current renewable energy sources into traditional alternating current power grids. Our team aims to create an inverter capable of synchronizing renewable sources and facilitating a new era in microgrid technologies. The InSync Power group plans to achieve this using already existing data from national utilities companies, artificial intelligence, power electronics, programmable microcontrollers, and a wide range of other techniques in order to create an easy-to-use and efficient soultion.
